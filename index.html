About IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of CHRIST (Deemed to be University) works continuously towards enhancing and sustaining the quality of academics, administration, and research through structured initiatives and active participation of all stakeholders.

Objectives of IQAC

Developing a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic action to improve performance.
Promoting institutional functioning towards quality enhancement and best practices.
Ensuring timely and efficient performance of academic, administrative, and financial tasks.
Maintaining relevance and quality in academic and research programmes.
Ensuring equitable access and affordability of academic programmes.
Integrating modern methods of teaching and learning.
Enhancing the credibility of evaluation procedures.
Maintaining adequacy and proper allocation of support services.
Sharing research findings and networking with institutions in India and abroad.

Functions of IQAC

Developing and applying quality benchmarks/parameters for academic and administrative activities.
Facilitating a learner-centric environment and faculty development for participatory teaching.
Collecting feedback from students, parents, and stakeholders on institutional processes.
Disseminating information on quality parameters of higher education.
Organizing workshops, seminars, and promoting quality circles.
Documenting activities leading to quality improvement.
Coordinating quality-related activities and disseminating best practices.
Maintaining institutional database through MIS for quality enhancement.

IQAC Committees

Academic Excellence Committee

Purpose: Monitor academic performance, improve curriculum, and enhance research quality.

Student Welfare Committee

Purpose: Ensure student development, mentorship programs, and feedback collection.

Research & Innovation Committee

Purpose: Promote research initiatives, innovation projects, and publications.

Quality Assurance Committee

Purpose: Maintain NAAC/Accreditation standards and institutional quality records.

Community Outreach Committee

Purpose: Organize social service, outreach programs, and NGO collaborations.

Faculty Development Committee

Purpose: Conduct workshops, training, and skill enhancement for faculty members.

About IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of Christ (Deemed to be University) was established in 2001 as per NAAC guidelines. It works towards continuous quality improvement, institutionalizing best practices, and promoting academic excellence through participatory approaches.

IQAC Committees
